Place

Data is processed at the Owner's operating offices and in any other places where the processing parties are located.

Detailed Information on Trackers

Essential Trackers

These are necessary for the website to function:

  • Session cookies (expire when browser closes)
  • Authentication cookies

Analytics Trackers

These help us understand how visitors interact:

  • Google Analytics (Google LLC) - Usage Data
  • Hotjar (Hotjar Ltd) - Behavior analytics

Advertising Trackers

Used for interest-based advertising:

  • Facebook Pixel (Facebook, Inc.) - Trackers +2
  • Google Ads Conversion Tracking - Trackers

How to Opt Out of Tracker Collection

Users can manage preferences for Trackers directly within their device settings.

For example:

  • Browser settings to block cookies
  • Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals
  • Platform-specific opt-outs (e.g., Facebook, Google)

Retention Time

Trackers are processed and stored for as long as required:

  • Session cookies: Until browser closure
  • Persistent cookies: Up to 2 years
  • Analytics data: Up to 26 months

Information for EU Users

Under GDPR, users have the right to:

  • Withdraw consent for non-essential Trackers
  • Access information about stored Trackers
  • Request deletion of Tracker data

Information for US Users

Under state privacy laws, users can:

  • Opt-out of "sale" of personal information
  • Opt-out of targeted advertising
  • Limit use of sensitive information

Definitions

  • Tracker: Any technology that enables tracking (cookies, pixels, fingerprinting)
  • Cookie: Small data file stored in the user's device
  • First-party Tracker: Set by the website being visited
  • Third-party Tracker: Set by domains other than the website being visited
